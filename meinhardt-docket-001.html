Supplementary Specification Regarding a Previously Registered Complaint

Ní litir fhoirmiúil ghearáin atá sa teachtaireacht seo. Is bealach áisiúil é seo chun ábhair a roghnú de láimh—chun cabhrú le hosmosis an ábhair a leagann béim ar an ngearán foirmiúil a cuireadh isteach ar 13 Bealtaine 2025 agus dátaí ina dhiaidh.

This is not a formal complaint letter. It is a curated supplement—material that reinforces and contextualises the formal complaints previously registered, presented here as an accessible dossier entry for institutional and public record.

Core Position
The matter enclosed is not born of grievance, but of duty. It addresses a vacancy—not of power, but of remit. The complaint spans multiple administrations and institutional layers, tracing a continued failure to respond through successive roles of the Taoiseach, Tánaiste, Ministers, and senior public officials.
Breiseán · Comhthéacs Reachtach agus Polaitiúil

Tá sé tábhachtach a lua gur theip ar an Stát go córasach an dualgas reachtúil a ailíniú leis an dualgas reachtach, go háirithe i gcomhthéacs prótacal a leanadh amach anseo de réir cinneadh ábhartha ó Sheanad Éireann i mí Aibreáin 2020—le leanúint an phrótacail i rith na haistrithe—go raibh rochtain, freagracht agus leanúnachas feasachta ag na baill reachtúla araon.

The State has systematically failed to align its statutory duty with its legislative duty—particularly in the context of a protocol subsequently followed per a material decision of Seanad Éireann. That decision is a matter of public record and demonstrates the prior knowledge and awareness of legislative members at the time.

"No future generation may say, as too many before have had to: 'The warning came. And the system had no place to put it.'"
Achoimre Clúdaigh · Cover Summary
  • Is teip struchtúrtha rialachais í neamh-aitheantas an Stáit ar Taighde Oibríochtúil (Operations Research)—le himpleachtaí dúbailte ó thaobh cosanta agus úsáid shibhialta de.
  • The State's non-recognition of Operations Research constitutes a structural governance failure—with dual implications for defence and civil application.
  • Níl sé laistigh de remit SIPO, agus tá an comhlacht sin tar éis an t-ábhar a atreorú ar bhonn próisis.
  • No committee or government department has accepted statutory responsibility for the matter, despite it being central to public strategy and ethical law.
  • Léiríonn cinneadh Sheanad Éireann i mí Aibreáin 2020 go raibh rochtain, freagracht agus leanúnachas feasachta ag na baill reachtúla.

Dá réir sin, is é atá i gceist ná riosca poiblí gan dídine institiúideach—ábhar atá curtha ar leataobh go struchtúrtha, seachas gan fhios. A public risk without institutional shelter—structurally sidelined, not merely overlooked.

The Structural Argument · Remit Displacement and Democratic Accountability

The presentation demonstrates that this matter lies not only outside SIPO's remit, but also outside the operational scope of every parliamentary committee and government department—making it an exemplary case of what the Meinhardt corpus terms Remit Displacement: a public risk that has no institutional address.

The problem does not reside in the disciplines themselves—Operations Research, Decision Science, and Formal Organisation Theory are directly relevant to State functioning, national planning, institutional accountability, and public decision-making systems. The failure lies in the refusal to recognise and deploy them at government level.

"Nuair a bhíonn an fhaisnéis curtha i láthair go soiléir, agus fós níl aon struchtúr réidh chun freagracht a ghlacadh—is é sin teip remite."
When information is presented clearly, and still no structure is ready to accept responsibility—that is a remit failure.
On the Nature of the State as System
Ag an bpointe seo, is fiú a admháil: ní mór dúinn a bheith cúramach gan an Stát a léiriú mar mheaisín meicniúil a imoibríonn go díreach le faisnéis. Ní meicníocht líneach í an chumhacht phoiblí—ina mbíonn eolas = gníomh—ach córas níos casta, idirghníomhach, uaireanta féinchosanta.

Public power is not a linear mechanism where knowledge = action. It is a more complex, interactive, sometimes self-defensive system. The institutional corpus can receive information without responding, transform it, defer it, or use it in ways that do not benefit the public.
On Archive and Action · The Secretary-as-Archivist

Each document had an immediate diplomatic purpose when created, but now each carries lasting scholarly and legal value. The Secretary-as-Archivist bears the living link between the immediate purpose of the office and the historical foundation that sustains continuity.

Is í seo an tsamhail atá le forbairt: comhfhorbairt chomhuaineach idir taidhleoireacht agus cartlannaíocht, idir comhairle dlí agus doiciméadú eiticiúil, idir seirbhís phoiblí agus stair phoiblí. The model to be developed: simultaneous co-development between diplomacy and archiving, between legal counsel and ethical documentation, between public service and public history.

Is dá bharr sin a éilítear anois samhail níos dinimiciúla de rialachas—samhail a aithníonn nach mbíonn gníomh i gcónaí mar thoradh ar fhios. A more dynamic model of governance is now required: one that recognises that action does not always follow from knowledge. Often, procedure itself is the instrument of evasion.

Príomhchoincheapa · Key Concepts in the Meinhardt Corpus
Concept · I
Nullification Loop
The structural circuit in which a submission is received, acknowledged, and deflected without resolution—completing a loop that produces the appearance of engagement without accountability.
Concept · II
Remit Displacement
The condition in which a matter of public significance has no institutional address—not overlooked by accident, but structurally excluded from the remit of every available body.
Concept · III
Interregnum Nullificans
The governance interregnum in which formal structures are operative but functionally dormant—neither admitting nor resolving the matter before them.
Concept · IV
Designed Forgetting
The active suppression of institutional memory through procedural means—transparency law, administrative succession, and digital disorganisation as instruments of erasure.
Concept · V
Finance of Notice
The cost—institutional, legal, civic—of formally placing a matter on record. The corpus argues this cost has been borne entirely by the claimant, not the State.
Concept · VI
Tairiscint Mheinhardt
The formal proposal at the centre of the corpus—an offer to integrate Operations Research methodology into Irish and EU governance, submitted and systematically unaddressed.
Dual-Use Classification · 26kg Missing Research
Privacy and Property — Príobháideachas agus Cosaint Maoin
The claim concerns: 26kg of dual-use research missing—weight equivalent to an Attic talent—including paramilitary intelligence, sensitive strategic knowledge, and high-risk governance methodologies; individuals who were directly or indirectly involved and subsequently disappeared in the context of institutional failure; a consular advisory index with cross-border risk and international obligations; and international safe harbour provisions regarding data protection, intellectual security, and State obligations in dual-use contexts.

Is é bunús an éilimh ná go bhfuil an dualgas aire (duty of care) ina fheidhm dhinimiciúil: ní hamháin gur gá freagracht nuair atá eolas ar fáil, ach go bhfuil leibhéal an chúraim a theastaíonn cóimheárach le méid, nádúr agus tábhacht an ábhair atá i gceist.

The basis of the claim is that the duty of care is a dynamic function: not merely that accountability is required when knowledge is available, but that the level of care required is proportional to the scale, nature, and significance of the matter involved.

Nochtadh Poiblí · Public Disclosure Statement
Registration and Rights
Chun críche taifead poiblí agus caidrimh phoiblí, tá an t-aighneacht seo á chlárú freisin mar iontráil shoiléir, oscailte, le go mbeidh sé intuigthe go forleathan go bhfuil an t-ábhar seo á phlé go gníomhach ag leibhéal institiúideach. For the purposes of public record and public relations, this submission is also being registered as a clear, open entry—so that it is widely understood that this matter is being actively discussed at institutional level.

I gcomhthéacs na haighneachta seo, atá á déanamh le haghaidh clárúcháin phoiblí agus chun leas trédhearcachta i gcúrsaí preasa, éilím go mbeidh mo phríobháideachas, agus mo chearta pearsanta agus intleachtúla faoi chosaint dhlíthiúil agus eiticiúil.

In the context of this submission, made for purposes of public registration and in the interest of press transparency, the claim is made that privacy, personal rights, and intellectual rights remain under legal and ethical protection—including research currently missing, the consular advisory index, and international provisions regarding safe harbour obligations.

Go hachomair: i bhfianaise nádúr dé-úsáide na teicneolaíochta atá i gceist, ba mhór agam é dá bhféadfaí mo ainm a choinneáil as aon phoiblíocht a d'fhéadfadh teacht as aon phróiseas iniúchta. Ní hé seo éalú, ach cosaint ar chearta i gcomhthéacs mífheidhmiú atá buan, córasach, agus cáipéiseach.

"Sa todhchaí, nuair a scrúdófar cá seasann an Stát i leith freagracht agus fáilte roimh fhaisnéis ríthábhachtach, beidh an t-ábhar seo ina tháscaire soiléir ar an méid a tugadh faoi deara, a tuigeadh."
In the future, when scrutinising where the State stands regarding accountability and welcome of critical information, this material will be a clear indicator of what was noticed, understood.
